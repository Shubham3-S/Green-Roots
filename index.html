About

GreenRoots was established on a bright spring day in April 2010, born from a shared passion for environmental sustainability and a vision of greener, healthier communities. What began as a small group of nature enthusiasts planting trees in local parks has blossomed into a thriving movement dedicated to reforestation and ecological balance.



Since our inception, GreenRoots has aimed to create a greener tomorrow. Today, we stand as a beacon of hope, working with volunteers and partners to plant trees, nurture biodiversity, and combat climate change. Join us in this green revolution, where every tree planted shapes a brighter, greener future for all.
